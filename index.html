Record Expungement in California

Who is eligible for record expungement?

Eligibility includes individuals who completed probation and did not serve time in state prison. Certain offenses may be excluded.

Certificate of Rehabilitation

What is a Certificate of Rehabilitation?

A court order declaring a person has been rehabilitated following a conviction and may assist with a governor's pardon.

Arrest Record Sealing

Can I seal an arrest that did not lead to charges?

Yes, under Penal Code 851.87, you may petition to seal arrests not resulting in conviction.

Early Probation Termination

How can I request early termination of probation?

You must file a motion with the court and demonstrate good conduct and compliance with all terms.
